Welcome to The Marist College Gym Crowd Meter

With our page, you can track, monitor, and ensure a great workout at any of the gyms and athletic centers on campus. Record and monitor the population at given areas on campus, (Mcann, Marketplace, etc.) and check to see when the best time to work out is. No one likes waiting for machines or benches, so make sure to check in daily to see how crowded your favorite workout spot is.

How does it work?

The Marist Crowd Meter will determine how many IDs are scanned into the athletic centers on campus and provide useful information in written and visual ways so you, the user, can make the best decision on when to go workout to get the best experience possible here on campus.

Where can I go?

On the different pages on our site, you can see individual and frequently updated statistics on different types of information on each location on campus.
